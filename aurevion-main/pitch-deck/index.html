13 — DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Built for trust, not engagement.

01

Visible uncertainty

Confidence bands on TDEE graphs. Auto-fit deviation on macro plans. We show what the system doesn't know — and how it learns.

02

Delta-first interfaces

The fridge shows what's missing, not what you have. The shopping list shows the gap. Every screen answers "what changed?" before "what is?"

03

Parallel, not sequential

Meal prep is a Gantt chart. Cooking is concurrent. The UI reflects the actual physics of a kitchen — multiple burners, one cook.

04

Mono-instrumentation aesthetic

Dark surfaces. Monospace labels. Hatching patterns for filled states. Data is the visual language — not gradients, illustrations, or mascots.
