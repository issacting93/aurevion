Design Principles

Built for trust, not engagement.

01

Visible uncertainty

Confidence bands on TDEE graphs. Auto-fit deviation on macro plans. We show what the system doesn't know — and how it learns.

02

Delta-first interfaces

The fridge shows what's missing, not what you have. The shopping list shows the gap. Every screen answers "what changed?"

03

Parallel, not sequential

Meal prep is a Gantt chart. Cooking is concurrent. The UI reflects the actual physics of a kitchen.

04

Mono-instrumentation

Dark surfaces. Monospace labels. Hatching patterns. Data is the visual language — not gradients, illustrations, or mascots.
