07 · IRIDESCENT JEWEL

Brand moment. One animated jewel for the screens that matter.

Rotating conic-gradient ring locked to our orange + white, glossy dark pill, soft glow spill. Too distinct for every screen, but exactly right for the screens that carry the brand: tab-bar home, start workout, open cook mode, sign on. Use sparingly — one per screen at most.
